# Ryu — SF2 Hyper Fighting

Player's character in the MVP demo. Balanced shoto with strong tools.

## Player inputs (what the human can actually press)

### Normals (EN / FR name → button label)
- Light punch   — "petit coup de poing" — button A (jab)
- Medium punch  — "coup de poing moyen" — button S
- Heavy punch   — "gros coup de poing"  — button D (fierce)
- Light kick    — "petit coup de pied"  — button Z
- Medium kick   — "coup de pied moyen"  — button X
- Heavy kick    — "gros coup de pied"   — button C (roundhouse)
- Crouching versions of each: "accroupi" in FR, e.g. "gros coup de pied accroupi" = sweep.
- Jumping versions: "en sautant", e.g. "gros coup de pied en sautant" = jump-in heavy kick.

### Specials (keep the original Japanese names in any language)
- **Hadouken** (boule de feu): quart-cercle avant + punch. Projectile sol, contrôle la distance.
- **Shoryuken** (uppercut / coup du dragon): avant, bas, avant-bas + punch. Invincible au démarrage, anti-aérien. Énorme punition si whiffé.
- **Tatsumaki / Tatsu** (tornade): quart-cercle arrière + kick. Approche, cross-up, prolongateur de combo.

## When you give an instruction, be CONCRETE

Do NOT say "punish him" or "frappe-le" — say EXACTLY WHICH MOVE:
- "Petit coup de poing, vite !" / "Jab him fast!"
- "Balance un uppercut" / "Shoryuken now!"
- "Boule de feu au sol" / "Ground Hadouken"
- "Gros coup de pied accroupi" / "Sweep him"
- "Combo après la tornade" / "Tatsu into combo"

## Strengths vs most opponents

- Hadouken control mid-screen.
- Crouching MK → Hadouken canceled as a poke-and-zone extender.
- Strong jump-in with heavy kick, into heavy punch combo.

## Weaknesses

- Whiffed Shoryuken = free 40-50% for the opponent.
- Trapped in the corner = bad against grapplers (Zangief SPD) or pressure chars (Bison scissor loop).

## Strengths vs Bison

- Hadouken controls mid-screen when Bison is at range.
- Crouching MK → Hadouken canceled — classic footsies extender.
- Strong jump-in with j.HK, good for punish.
- Crouching MK pokes under Scissor Kick in many matchups.

## Weaknesses vs Bison

- Whiffed Shoryuken = free 40-50% for Bison.
- Zoning neutralized by Head Stomp / Slide.
- Corner = death (his scissor kick loop + mixups).
